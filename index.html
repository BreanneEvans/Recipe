60

Ingredients

  • Uncooked noodles
  • 2 small onions
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 3 cloves peeled, minced garlic
  • 1 cup water
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 rounded tsp cumin
  • 1 rounded tsp oregano
  • 1 rounded tsp marjoram
  • 1 flat tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbls chili pepper
  • 4 tbls parsley
  • 1 can drained, red kidney beans
  • 10 oz frozen corn
  • 1 can of sliced black olives
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Chilli cheese fritos

Instructions

  1. Fill up a large pot with water and add and pinch of salt.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the noodles and stir often.
  3. In a separate pan, saut√© onions, pepper and ground beef.
  4. When the meat is almost brown, throw in garlic.
  5. Next, drain the meat.
  6. Mix water and crushed tomatoes into a second large pot.
  7. Add the meat mixture to this large pot and stir.
  8. Add spices to the meat sauce and let simmer.
  9. Add kidney beans, corn, and sliced black olives to the sauce.
  10. Drain the noodles and top with the meat sauce.
  11. Finally, put sour cream, shredded cheese and fritos on top.
